Donate Blood, Save Lives


Why Donate Blood?

Every donation can save up to three lives. Donating blood is a simple way to make a significant impact on the lives of patients in need.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Be at least 18 years old (or 17 with parental consent in some states)
  • Weigh at least 50 kgs
  • Be in good health
